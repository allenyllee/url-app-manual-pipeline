\section{Scope}
This manual currently covers the visible primary controls on the YouTube home page (desktop, signed-out state), including:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Top navigation bar (logo, search, voice search, sign in)
  \item Main links in the left navigation panel
  \item Clickable areas in video cards on the home feed
\end{itemize}

\section{Prerequisites}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Browser can access \texttt{https://www.youtube.com/}
  \item Recommended viewport width: at least 1280 px
  \item UI may differ by locale, language, and login status
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Top Navigation}
\begin{longtable}{p{0.22\linewidth} p{0.22\linewidth} p{0.48\linewidth}}
\toprule
Control & Type & Function \\
\midrule
\endhead
Hamburger Menu (top-left) & Button & Expands or collapses the left navigation panel. \\
YouTube Logo & Link & Returns to the home page (\texttt{/}). \\
Search Input & Input field & Accepts keywords for searching videos, channels, and playlists. \\
Search (Magnifier) & Button & Submits the search query and opens results page. \\
Voice Search (Mic) & Button & Starts voice input search (browser microphone permission required). \\
Sign In & Link/Button & Opens Google account sign-in flow. \\

\section{Example Task Flows}
\subsection{Flow A: Search for a Video}
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Enter keywords in the search input.
  \item Click the magnifier button.
  \item Review the search results page.
\end{enumerate}
\screenshotbox{images/flow-search-step1.png}{Flow A-1: Enter search keywords}{Capture the query entered in top search input}
\screenshotbox{images/flow-search-step2.png}{Flow A-2: Search results page}{Capture results list with query visible}

\subsection{Flow B: Open a Video Watch Page}
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Find any video card on the home feed.
  \item Click its thumbnail or title.
  \item Confirm the watch page and player are loaded.
\end{enumerate}
